Rizwan Go Rides 🚕

A simple bilingual taxi reservation website for Rizwan Go Rides, a local cab service based in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, India.

About the Project

Rizwan Go Rides provides reliable and comfortable taxi services through advance booking. The business currently operates two taxis and focuses on punctual, safe, and personalized travel service.

Customers can visit the website, choose their preferred language (English or Hindi), and submit a ride reservation request.

Features

Language selection (English / हिंदी)

Simple and mobile-friendly design

Online ride reservation form

Date picker calendar

Pickup and destination entry

Preferred travel time selection

Booking requests sent directly to email
